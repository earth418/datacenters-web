In 2023, AI occupied a small fraction of the tech industry.

By 2025, the industry's largest players total a market cap of $20 trillion.

With companies like OpenAI making double and evne triple-digit billion dollar deals for data centers,

What is all this money really doing?

The city of Tuscon, AZ has been, for the last several months, in battle with the interests of these private corporations about a proposed data center within their city limits.

This is Project Blue -- 290 acres set aside by the county to build a powerful data center.

After city residents voted against the data center, citing environmental, water use, and energy concerns, the municipality simply decided to let the company build the data center outside city limits.
